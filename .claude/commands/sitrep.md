---
description: "Generate a situation report of the project's current status"
---

Read the entire project structure and progress.md, then produce a SITREP (Situation Report) covering:

1. **Mission Status** — What phase of development we're in
2. **Completed Objectives** — Features that are implemented and tested
3. **Active Operations** — What's currently being worked on
4. **Blocked/Issues** — Any problems or failures
5. **Next Orders** — Recommended next steps

Format as a military-style briefing. Be concise.
